I am a Business Analytics graduate from Macquarie University with a foundation in Applied Statistics, originally from India and now based in Sydney. My background bridges data and commercial decision making, spanning FMCG category management, retail analytics, and business development across two countries, giving me a perspective that sits across both the analytical and commercial sides of a business.

I am most drawn to roles where data tells a story that actually changes what a business does next. Outside of work I am drawn to the outdoors, hiking new trails, discovering hidden corners of Sydney, and chasing new adventures. I believe curiosity outside of work feeds the same instinct that drives good analytical thinking professionally.
